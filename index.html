About Me

I'm Shadrick Chilongo, an Information Security Analyst with diplomas in Ethical Hacking & Cybersecurity (Alison) and a large portion of my expertise built through self-driven, hands-on projects. I focus on identifying vulnerabilities, strengthening defenses, and ensuring systems remain resilient under pressure.

Rather than only following a syllabus, I solve real problems: building labs, running pentests, analyzing logs, and documenting remediation. This practical path has made me adaptable, resourceful and effective at translating technical risks into clear action for teams.

Goal: Join a forward-thinking security team where I can contribute my toolset, curiosity and problem-solving approach to protect people and data.
